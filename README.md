\# WFSL Device Health Guard



Deterministic device health validation and compliance guard for WFSL systems.



WFSL Device Health Guard evaluates the operational health, configuration

integrity, and compliance posture of engineering workstations and managed

devices used within WFSL-governed environments.



All assessments are deterministic and derived from declared checks,

verified system state, and WFSL governance rules.



---



\## Capabilities



\- Deterministic validation of device health and readiness

\- Verification of declared system configuration and compliance state

\- Explicit PASS / FAIL outcomes suitable for audit and replay

\- Structured outputs for governance and verification pipelines

\- Integration with WFSL evidence and admission systems



---



\## Scope



WFSL Device Health Guard operates at the \*\*device inspection boundary\*\* only.



It does \*\*not\*\*:

\- Perform automated remediation

\- Execute arbitrary system changes

\- Collect personal data

\- Transmit telemetry without governance approval

\- Perform behavioural monitoring



This component evaluates declared health state and reports deterministically.



---



\## Intended Users



\- WFSL engineering environments

\- Licensed WFSL services

\- Internal compliance, readiness, and verification pipelines



This component is not intended for consumer use or unmanaged environments.
